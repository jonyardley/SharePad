Get set up in a minute

  1. Open the DMG and drag SharePad into Applications.
  2. Launch it and find SharePad in your menu bar, not the Dock.
  3. Plug in your iPad over USB and tap Trust. The share window appears automatically.
  4. Pick the SharePad window in any call's "Share window" list, and you're live.
